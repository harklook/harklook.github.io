Tracking inventory, responding to demand shifts, and avoiding losses from overstocking or dead stock — these are daily battles for independent retailers. And they're fighting them alone.

02 Our Solution

Every Store, Connected.

Jiran connects retailers into a unified, intelligent network. It combines a centralized B2B marketplace with real-time inventory tracking, allowing stores to buy and sell excess stock within a trusted local ecosystem.

Stores unified — real-time data, shared network
03 Core Features

FIVE TOOLS. ONE PLATFORM.

B2B Inventory Exchange

Trade excess stock with nearby retailers instead of letting it go to waste.

Demand Forecasting using AI

Real-Time Inventory

Instantly track stock levels and product availability across stores.

Dead Stock Detection

Identify slow-moving items early and take action before losses grow.

04 Why Jiran?

ENTERPRISE POWER. CORNER STORE SIMPLICITY.

Jiran is built specifically for general stores. It focuses on simplicity, speed, and real-world usability — giving small retailers access to tools typically reserved for large-scale operations.

Our vision is to unify independent stores into one intelligent inventory network, turning fragmentation into collective strength.
